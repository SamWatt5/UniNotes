# 1. Introduction
- **Pentatonic** = "five tones"
- One of the most widely used scales in rock, blues, pop, metal, funk, jazz, country
- Favoured because:
	- No half steps (avoids strong dissonance)
	- Shapes are easy to learn and movable
	- Works over many chord progressions
# 2. Minor Pentatonic Scale
**Formula (relative to minor scale)**
1, b3, 4, 5, b7
**In A minor**
A - C - D - E - G - (A)
