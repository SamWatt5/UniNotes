### Week 1: The Musical Alphabet & Fretboard Basics

- **Topics:** Notes (A‚ÄìG), sharps/flats, octave shapes, natural notes on low E and A strings.

### Week 3: The Pentatonic Scale (Your Improvisation Toolbox)

- **Topics:** Minor pentatonic, blues scale, box 1 pattern.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Learn A minor pentatonic across the neck.
        
    - Create 2-bar improvisations over a backing track.
        
- **Guitar application:** Jam over a 12-bar blues in Am.
    

### Week 4: Chords 101

- **Topics:** Triads (major, minor, diminished), power chords.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Build chords from C major scale.
        
    - Play triads on strings 1-3, 2-4, 3-5.
        
- **Guitar application:** Recognize chord tones inside your pentatonic scale.

- **Topics:** Key signatures, relative major/minor.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Write out all chords in the key of C and G.
        
    - Play the I‚ÄìIV‚ÄìV progression in 3 keys.
        
- **Guitar application:** Practice common progressions (C‚ÄìF‚ÄìG, G‚ÄìC‚ÄìD).
    

### Week 6: Chord Progressions

- **Topics:** Diatonic harmony (I ii iii IV V vi vii¬∞).

## **Stage 3 ‚Äì Advanced Concepts (Weeks 9‚Äì12)**

**Goal:** Build ear training, songwriting, and improvisation skills.

### Week 9: Ear Training & Transcription

- **Topics:** Interval recognition, chord quality by ear.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Sing + play intervals daily.
        
    - Transcribe a simple riff by ear.
        
- **Guitar application:** Learn solos without tabs.
    

### Week 10: Rhythm & Meter

- **Topics:** Note values, syncopation, polyrhythms.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Clap subdivisions (quarter, eighth, triplet, sixteenth).
        
    - Play funk rhythms on muted strings.
        
- **Guitar application:** Groove-based riff writing.
    

### Week 11: Modulation & Songwriting

- **Topics:** Key changes, borrowed chords, secondary dominants.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Write an 8-bar progression with a modulation.
        
    - Try using bVII chord in a major key.
        
- **Guitar application:** Write a short song with chord progression & melody.
    

### Week 12: Improvisation Mastery

- **Topics:** Mixing scales, phrasing, call & response.
    
- **Exercises:**
    
    - Solo using pentatonic + major scale notes.
        
    - Record & analyze your soloing.
        
- **Guitar application:** Improvise over different genres (blues, rock, funk, jazz).
